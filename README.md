### 🎓 Research & PhD

I am a PhD candidate at the **[DAVID Laboratory](https://www.david.uvsq.fr/)**, a joint research unit of **UVSQ (Université de Versailles Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines)** and **Université Paris-Saclay**.

My research focuses on **Machine Learning and Meta-Optimization**: developing a hybrid approach for operational research on the **Vehicle Routing Problem (VRP)**.

- 🐍 **Languages:** Python (Mainly)
- 🔥 **Deep Learning:** PyTorch
- 🛠️ **Focus:** Meta-heuristics, Optimization, Deep Reinforcement Learning
